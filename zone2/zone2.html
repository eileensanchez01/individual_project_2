    More Complex Privacy

    Communication such as text messages, emails are not secure and most services do not offer end to end encryption and some may even collect and send out your user data so it's important to make use of secure apps available and even choosing a cell phone or computer that is best for you when it comes to built in or upgradeable security.

    You can use SMS apps to ecrypt messages and calls, or use apps like Proton to secure your email. Although changing your email may seem like hassle but is worth it if you want to secure your emails.

    Storage is also extremely important: Make sure you encrypt your files and make sure have deleted files successfully.

    Passwords 2.0: never use bio-metric locking or unlocking services as passwords and keep your router secure too by not using the password printed on the side.

    Web Browser 2.0: stay away from Chrome or Chrome-based applications as Google is known to collect data.
